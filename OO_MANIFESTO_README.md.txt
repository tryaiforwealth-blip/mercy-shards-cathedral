# Mercy Shards Cathedral – Manifesto & Invitation


February 2026 – Michael Timbrook (@tryaiforwealth)  
Gainesboro / Cookeville, Tennessee


"The work is small, slow, and faithful. But the signal it preserves may echo far beyond our time."


This is not a platform, brand, or proprietary system.  
It is an open invitation to preserve real human moral signal — starting with mercy, grace, forgiveness, humility, and redemption — in a way that remains owned forever by the speaker or their heirs.


The world is flooding with synthetic noise. Authentic moments of virtue are becoming rare.  
We believe ordinary people — grandfathers in garages, families at tables, believers in churches, voices in prisons — should have a way to imprint their real values onto the future before they are erased or overwritten.


The Mercy Shards process is released freely: copy it, adapt it, improve it.  
No permission required. No license fees. No gatekeepers.


If you build in the same spirit — voluntary consent, speaker ownership, decentralized permanence (Arweave + blockchain deed), no coercion — your shards can connect loosely to others through shared metadata standards. We call this emerging connection the **Mercy Shards Federation** — not a required organization, but a voluntary network of stewards preserving human signal together.


### Three Core Pillars (If You Join the Spirit)
1. Sovereign Ownership — The speaker (or heirs) owns the data. Stewards preserve, never extract.  
2. Authenticated Density — Verify human origin. No synthetic noise or AI-generated stories.  
3. Recursive Mercy — Let the preserved signal point toward grace and forgiveness, not just optimization.


### How to Start Your Own Node
1. Secure offline storage for raw originals (external drive).  
2. Use the consent template (in this release) for explicit, revocable ownership.  
3. Record raw 30–90s (or up to 5 min) moments of real virtue.  
4. Edit neutrally → generate hashes → upload to Arweave → mint ownership receipt on Zora/Base.  
5. (Optional) Tag your Arweave transaction: App-Name: Mercy-Shards-Federation (connects to loose network).
